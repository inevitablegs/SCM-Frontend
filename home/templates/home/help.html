Help Center

Find answers to your questions about the Supply Chain Portal

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Click on either "Manufacturer Register" or "Supplier Register" on the homepage, depending on your role. Fill out the registration form with your details and follow the instructions to complete your account setup.
What's the difference between manufacturer and supplier accounts?
Manufacturers are companies looking for suppliers to provide materials or components. Suppliers are companies offering materials, components, or services to manufacturers.

Account Management

How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your registered email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password.
Can I change my account type after registration?
No, account types cannot be changed after registration. You'll need to create a new account with the correct type.

Platform Features

How do I find suppliers/manufacturers?
After logging in, use the search functionality in your dashboard to find potential partners. You can filter by industry, location, and other criteria.
Is there a messaging system?
Yes, once you've connected with a partner through the platform, you can use our built-in messaging system to communicate securely.
